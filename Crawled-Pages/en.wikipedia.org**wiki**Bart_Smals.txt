	Bart Smals
		From Wikipedia, the free encyclopedia

		Bart Smals (born 5 April 1970) is a Dutch politician, currently serving as a Member of Parliament for the People's Party for Freedom and Democracy (Volkspartij voor Vrijheid en Democratie) since 3 July 2019.[1][2]
