The Roman Catholic Diocese of Ciudad Valles (Latin: Dioecesis Vallipolitana) (erected 27 November 1960) is a suffragan diocese of the Archdiocese of San Luis Potosí. It was a suffragan of the Archdiocese of Monterrey until 25 November 2006.
