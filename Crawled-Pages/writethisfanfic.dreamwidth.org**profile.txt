                 Mini Bio
                writethisfanfic is a community for helping you with working on your fan fiction. We aim to provide support and resources for everyone. Introduce yourself, comment on any of the daily check in posts, volunteer. During the first week of the month we have a challenge sign up post for those who need to work towards something, this is optional but some members find this useful. We also have a volunteers sign up post for helping out with hosting the daily check in post, these generally run Sunday to Saturday with Saturday been the day for sharing snippets and seeking help.These recurring monthly posts are always the community sticky posts, links are provided to the previous months posts when these are updated.Promoting other writing communities and challenges – this is absolutely fine, please let me know first.We have a webchat channel www.slashnet.org/webclient/writethisfanfic but I don’t know how active this is or even if it’s still been used by members.Any problems or suggestions for improvement, please contact your mods: lilly_c (Cat) via PM.
