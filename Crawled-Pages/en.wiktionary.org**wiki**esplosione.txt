Italian[edit]
  L’esplosione di una bomba nucleare – The explosion of a nuclear bomb
Etymology[edit]
From Latin explōsiō, explōsiōnem, derived from explōdō (“I eject” ← “I drive out by clapping”).
Pronunciation[edit]
IPA(key): /es.ploˈzjo.ne/, [es̪pl̺oˈz̪joːn̺e]
Hyphenation: e‧splo‧sió‧ne
Noun[edit]
esplosione f (plural esplosioni)
Italian Wikipedia has an article on:esplosioneWikipedia it
explosion, blast, burst
Synonyms: detonazione, scoppio
Antonym: implosione
explosion (sound)
Synonyms: botto (colloquial), scoppio
(figurative) outburst, outbreak
Synonym: scoppio
(phonetics) release (of a plosive consonant)
