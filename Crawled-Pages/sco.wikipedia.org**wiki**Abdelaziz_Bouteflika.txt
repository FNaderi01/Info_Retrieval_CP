Abdelaziz Bouteflika (pronunciation (help·info); Arabic: عبد العزيز بوتفليقة‎ [ʕaːbd lʕziz butfliqa]; born 2 Mairch 1937) is an Algerian politeecian who haes been the fift Preses o Algerie (1999-2019).[1]
