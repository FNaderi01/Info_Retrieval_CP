French[edit]
Etymology[edit]
Inherited from Ecclesiastical Latin canonicus, a borrowing from Ancient Greek κανονικός (kanonikós). Doublet of canonique.
Pronunciation[edit]
IPA(key): /ʃa.nwan/
Noun[edit]
chanoine m (plural chanoines)
(religion) canon (person)
