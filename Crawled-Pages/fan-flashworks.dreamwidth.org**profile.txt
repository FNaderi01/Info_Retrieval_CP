                 Mini Bio
                Fan_FlashworksMultimedia Multifandom Flash ChallengesUSWe love it when fans make awesome stuff.We think deadlines are a great way to get the creative juices flowing.We love fanworks in every shape and form.We hope for this to be a community where people are encouraging and supportive of each other’s works.YOUYou can write, you can vid, you can bake, you can knit.You can just hang out. Kick back and browse.You can be any in any fandom, from huge megalithic ones to tiny fandoms of one.There's plenty of room here for everyone. We want you to join us.RULESWe have guidelines. Don't let them scare you, though.  We welcome newbies and lurkers and wanted to be thorough so nobody would be left flailing, not sure what to do. Here’s a rundown of the basics:Have fun. Flail. Squee. Love.Be nice.Draw, manip, write, vid, meta, bake, graph, poll or otherwise create your works to the current prompt within the challenge period.Follow mods’ cues and requests.Post your work to the comm (no fake cuts!) and do not post it anywhere else until we close the round. (Crossposting by linking back to the comm is totally cool though!)Use cut tags, hide NSFW images, rate NC-17 content and put up warnings when necessary.Keep the file size of uncut images below 100kb. Use thumbnails where appropriate.Post only completed works to the comm.Don’t lock your posts.Be proud of your achievements, be they creative or feedbacky.LOCATIONFan_flashworks is on livejournal and dreamwidth. Please don’t crosspost your works. When a challenge closes, the mods will post a complete index for the challenge to each comm.  All works will also be tagged on the community's pinboard.SCHEDULEChallenges will be held three times a month (roughly every ten days). The challenge prompt will be announced by the mods in an announcement post.Save late submissions for an amnesty round (every two months).DISCLAIMERAll works posted, linked, or promoted at this community are fan-created and fictitious. We don't own anything, we don't mean any harm, and we don't make any money.♥ THANKS ♥Thanks to jerakeen for letting us borrow many of the guidelines from kradamadness!Thanks to cherryice for helping us out with design stuff and making our awesome banners!And thanks to thefulcrum for creating our layout and making it available to use.
