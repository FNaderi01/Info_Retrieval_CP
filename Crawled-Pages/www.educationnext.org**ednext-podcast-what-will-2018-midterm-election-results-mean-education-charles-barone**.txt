EdNext Podcast · Ep. 142 – Dec. 5, 2018: What Will the 2018 Midterm Election Results Mean for Education?
			Charles Barone, the chief policy officer at Democrats for Education Reform and Education Reform Now, joins Education Next Editor-in-chief Marty West to discuss the results of the midterm election and what impact they might have on education policy.
Barone recently wrote “Election 2018: The Voters Have Spoken” for EdNext.
The EdNext Podcast is available on Apple Podcasts, Google Play, Soundcloud, Stitcher and here every Wednesday.
– Education Next
