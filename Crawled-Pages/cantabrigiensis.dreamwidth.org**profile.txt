                 Mini Bio
                This is a community for students/alumni of the University of Cambridge and for anyone else who is interested.Share your thoughts on supervisions, lectures, lecturers, colleges, exams, admissions, top-up fees, CUSU, boaties, oxford, ents, socs, formals, tutors,...anything!If you are a prospective applicant wondering how on earth to choose a college, have a look here before you post questions. Please make any questions you do post fairly specific - although we'd love to help you out, this community is primarily intended for current/past Cambridge students.General information about Oxbridge and also about previous applicants' experiences with interviews etc can be found at www.oxbridge-admissions.org.uk
