DescriptionCH-53 landing at Defense Attaché Office compound, Operation Frequent Wind.jpg
English: United States Marine Private First Class Forrest M. Turner, Jr. provides security as two Sikorsky CH-53 helicopters land at the Defense Attaché Office compound during Operation Frequent Wind. Military helicopters dropped the ground security component at landing zones. Once on the ground they set up security positions.

Military helicopters dropped Regimental Landing Team 4, the ground security component, at seven landing zones near the DAO compound, its headquarters building and annex. Once on the ground they moved to set up security positions. (Photo by Dirck Halstead) (released)
