                 Mini Bio
                This is the community for discussion of suggestions for improving Dreamwidth! You can't post directly to the community: if you have a suggestion, use the Suggestions Generator, which will gather all of the necessary information and put your suggestion into the moderation queue. We'll post the suggestions to this community for review, discussion, and voting.Having a suggestion get positive responses doesn't guarantee that it'll be implemented, but we've made it easy to migrate your suggestions into our bugtracking database, and if your suggestion gets chosen for migration, it will eventually get added to the site.
