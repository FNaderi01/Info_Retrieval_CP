		Santos FC, eyaziwa ngokuba Santos, It is a club eBrazil multi-kwezemidlalo izinze kwisixeko eselunxwemeni Santos. Isekelwe Aprili 14, 1912, waba ibhola omnye iiklabhu kakhulu ngempumelelo Brazil kunye elaziwa emhlabeni.[1][2] Santos waba idumileyo 60s yi izihloko zikazwelonke nezamazwe ngamazwe eziliqela oyiswe kwaye lokuthetha Pele, ingqalelo lomdlali ilungileyo kwinkulungwane ngu FIFA kananjalo waqhokra eyona mitsi mini kwimbali Santos iqela lesizwe eBrazil.[3][4]
Imibala akhe amhlophe namnyama, yaye kufuneka iayinwe iyunifomu yayo yemveli emhlophe ngokupheleleyo. Thumela imidlalo zabo kwibala yabo, i-Vila Belmiro, kodwa maxa wambi uye imidlalo yabo Pacaembu Stadium.[5]
