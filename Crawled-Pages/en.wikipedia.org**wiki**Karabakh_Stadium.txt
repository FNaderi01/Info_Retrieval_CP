The Azersun Arena (Azerbaijani: Azərsun Arena) is a football stadium in Yeni Suraxanı, Baku, Azerbaijan, opened in June 2015.[1][2] The main tenant of the stadium is Qarabağ, who moved from their home at the Tofiq Bahramov Stadium when it was completed.[3]
Construction[edit]
The stadium has a capacity of 5,800 and is based in Yukhari Surakhani.[4][1] The stadium opened in 2015.
