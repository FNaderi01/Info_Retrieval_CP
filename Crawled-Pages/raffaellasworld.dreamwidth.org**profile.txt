                 Mini Bio
                I'm a history geek, food geek, and generally obsessed with all things Venetian especially pre 1600's. I'm also on the board of directors for a non-profit called It's My Bag, that provides bags, toiletries and personal belongings for children in the foster care system. I believe life is all about choices, and I choose to try to add more positive to the world than negative. Favorite movies include Rosencrantz &amp; Guildenstern are Dead, Gothic &amp; most of the Dead series (day of the dead, dawn of the dead, etc.). I also really enjoy English comedy.
