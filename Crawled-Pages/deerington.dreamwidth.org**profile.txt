 A multifandom inspired dreamscape where characters explore the horrors and strangeness of their own worlds, and worlds unknown, all the while trying to solve one simple question: what is Deerington and why have they been brought here? This is a game that explores character psyche and the consequences of choices. Every flavor of horror can be found within this game, as every sub-genre offers a new perspective for the characters to explore. It isn't worth it to try and wake up. After all, your greatest cage will always be your own mind.deerington functions as the game's log community. The game plurk sodder is where you can keep up with game updates, clues, and other important announcements.
