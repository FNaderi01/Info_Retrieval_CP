CEPR reports
CEPR produces a range of Reports, often in collaboration with other research organisations. The collection includes the annual Geneva Reports on the World Economy (jointly with ICMB), as well as reports from Global Trade Alert, an initiative coordinated by CEPR that monitors policies that affect word trade, and the Monitoring International Integration reports.
