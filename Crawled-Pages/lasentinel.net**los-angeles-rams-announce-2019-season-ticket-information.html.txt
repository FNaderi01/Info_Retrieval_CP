Los Angeles Rams Announce 2019 Season Ticket Information            
				By Amanda Scurlock                
	                	                Published March 19, 2019
		Rams running back Todd Gurley (Robert Torrence/L.A. Sentinel)
The Los Angeles Rams announced the kickoff of 2019 Season Ticket renewals and new sales. Season tickets are now available on therams.com/tickets.
Season tickets are the best available locations throughout the Los Angeles Memorial Coliseum at the lowest price available. Rams Season Ticket Members receive exclusive benefits and access including savings over single game prices, access to playoff tickets at special prices, payment plans, free NFL RedZone on their mobile phones, special discounts and exclusive gameday experiences such as meeting Rams’ Legends or being on-field for pregame introductions.
        ADVERTISEMENT        
Starting today, 2018 Season Ticket Members will receive a communication about the opportunity to renew their seats for the upcoming season at therams.com/stmrenewals. Ticket holders that choose to renew their seats by Monday, April 1 will be eligible for the seat relocation program to upgrade their seats based on availability, will have the chance to win a free trip for two to the NFL Draft in Nashville, and can win daily prize giveaways and autographed merchandise. The Season Ticket Member renewal window will officially close on Friday, May 3.
 Additionally, fans interested in becoming new Season Ticket Members for the 2019 season can now view available seats at therams.com/seasontickets or by calling to speak with a Los Angeles Rams representative at 818-338-0011 extension 1. New Season Ticket Members can experience the excitement in-person as the defending NFC Champions celebrate their final season in the renovated Los Angeles Memorial Coliseum. Make lifetime memories for you and your family or friends by experiencing exclusive Season Ticket Member experiences such as attending private offseason workouts or being on-field before or after the game.
The 2019 Rams home schedule includes a rematch of last year’s NFC Championship game against the New Orleans Saints, as well as hosting the NFC North Champion Chicago Bears, the AFC North Champion Baltimore Ravens and the Tampa Bay Buccaneers. The Rams also will host their NFC West rivals the Seattle Seahawks, Arizona Cardinals and San Francisco 49ers. The dates and times of games will be announced by the NFL at a later date.
During the 2019 season, the Rams will play their home games at a renovated Los Angeles Memorial Coliseum. Upgraded features will include Wi-Fi connectivity, new stadium seats with cupholders, additional aisles, improved concession stands, more ADA seating and a new Scholarship Tower which includes a Press Box and Sky Deck, as well as premium seating options.
Los Angeles Rams Season Ticket Member benefits include:
Exclusive Season Ticket member trips and experiences, including to the Super Bowl, Pro Bowl and NFL Draft;
Special access at the Rams Draft party, offseason practices and Rams Training Camp at UC Irvine;
Special gameday experiences and upgrades, including pregame and halftime involvement;
Free NFL RedZone on your mobile phone and free NFL Game Pass to replay every NFL game of the season;
Season Ticket Member exclusive gift;
Autograph sessions with Rams Alumni
Paying less per game compared to single-game ticket buyers;
Flexible payment plan;
Playoff ticket priority;
15% Savings on RamsFanShop.com and merchandise in-stadium
For information about Rams tickets, fans may visit therams.com/tickets. Fans who are interested in purchasing season tickets for the Rams 2019 season also can call the Rams Ticket Office at 818-338-0011 X 1. Current Season Ticket Members who would like to discuss season ticket renewal for the upcoming season can call 818-338-0011 X 2.
