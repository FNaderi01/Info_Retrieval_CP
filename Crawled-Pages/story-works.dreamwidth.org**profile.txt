                 Mini Bio
                Welcome to Story Works, a comm that wants to inspire you to create :)This is a home to all sorts of fests and challenges that come to be when we put our heads together and think of ways to inspire your creative minds :) All creativity is welcomed, fannish or non-fannish, from stories to meta to graphics to crafts to anything you can imagine!Everyone is welcome to join, but being a member of the comm is not required to take part in anything. Come and go as you please. All we ask is that you play nice and respect others. And have fun! ^_^The rules are simple:1) Be nice and respectful of others - no bashing or hatred of any kind. This is the most important rule!2) Use a header when posting creations. Here's the header code:&lt;b&gt;Author/Artist:&lt;/b&gt;
&lt;b&gt;Title:&lt;/b&gt; 
&lt;b&gt;Fandom:&lt;/b&gt;
&lt;b&gt;Pairings(s):&lt;/b&gt;
&lt;b&gt;Rating:&lt;/b&gt;
&lt;b&gt;Word count:&lt;/b&gt; (for fics)
&lt;b&gt;Warnings/Tags:&lt;/b&gt; (add any major warnings, and tags as you see fit.)
3) Put all large posts and images under a cut.4) We also ask you to keep in mind, that for a lot of people fandom is a safe haven, so whatever you post please warn people about any triggering content the post might have, and put any triggering content under a cut. If aren't sure if something might be triggering or not, be on the safe side and warn for it. Thank you for understanding.If you have any questions about things, contact either me, selenic76, or melagan.Once again, welcome, and I hope we can inspire you ^_^
