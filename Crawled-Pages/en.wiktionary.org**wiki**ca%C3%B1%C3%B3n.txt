Spanish[edit]
Etymology[edit]
caño (“tube, pipe”) +‎ -ón (augmentative).
Pronunciation[edit]
IPA(key): /kaˈɲon/, [kaˈɲõn]
Rhymes: -on
Noun[edit]
cañón m (plural cañones)
(geography) canyon
cannon
barrel (of a gun), pipe, tube
