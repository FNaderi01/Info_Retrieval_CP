              Plan your next camping trip, manage event registrations, whip up a quick poll,

              Use your own photo or logo, and Forms will pick just the right colors to complete
              your own unique form, or choose from a set of curated themes to set the tone.
              Q&amp;A, your way
              Choose from a bunch of question options, from multiple choice to dropdowns to a
              linear scale. Add images and YouTube videos, or get fancy with page
              branching and question skip logic.
              Create or respond on the go
              Forms is responsive, so that means it’s easy (and beautiful) to make, edit, and
              respond to forms on screens big and small.

              time response info and charts. Or, take your data further by viewing it all in
              Sheets.
