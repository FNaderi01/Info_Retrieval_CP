Whether transforming a 1936 Ford Coupe for an almond farmer, or customizing a 1937 Zephr for a demanding millionaire, renovating cars for hoarders is a risky proposition.

                    Back-ground music..."Isn"t"
                        21 March 2014 | by smokeykavon
                        – See all my reviews
                        I just watched your show a few hours ago in my shop with several auto restoration friends. We thought it was pretty good. I liked the concept of finding a bunch of items then taking it all instead of just a few items. The Corvair 'chopped' look was great. The reason I gave you a low view rating was because your "back-ground" music was front-ground noise. Not only me, my friends said the same thing. Heavy 'metal' type of music tends to be more on the louder side and has a tendency to play on the eardrums differently. We could hardly hear what was said. Good luck with your show. Thank you...Bob, Jeff, Randy, Steve, Josh, &amp; Eli the Dog.
