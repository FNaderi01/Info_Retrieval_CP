Gary Anthony Wayne (born November 30, 1962) is a former Major League Baseball left-handed pitcher.

Early life[edit]
Wayne graduated from Crestwood High School in 1980.  He is an alumnus of the University of Michigan. In 1983, he played collegiate summer baseball in the Cape Cod Baseball League for the Yarmouth-Dennis Red Sox.[1]
Career[edit]
Drafted by the Montreal Expos in the 4th round of the 1984 MLB amateur draft, Wayne would make his Major League Baseball debut with the Minnesota Twins on April 7, 1989, and appeared in his final game on June 3, 1994.
Wayne was a member of the inaugural Colorado Rockies team that began play in Major League Baseball in 1993.
