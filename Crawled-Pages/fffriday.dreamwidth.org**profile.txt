                 Mini Bio
                Every Friday, review, recommend, or discuss an FF book, short story, or other work, and label the review "FF Friday." You can post on your own DW and put a link here, or post here - up to you.  You do not have to commit to doing this every Friday to join in. Whenever you can is fine.FF is here defined as a romantic relationship between female-identified people. The work does not have to be genre romance. It can be any genre that includes a significant and romantic relationship between women. It does not have to include sex, but should be clearly romantic rather than platonic friendship. Please use your own judgment as to what is romantic vs. platonic.Reviews do not have to be positive. They can be any length, down to one sentence. Personal abuse of commenters or authors is not allowed. Disagreement and negative reviews are fine, but personal abuse and insults are banned.
