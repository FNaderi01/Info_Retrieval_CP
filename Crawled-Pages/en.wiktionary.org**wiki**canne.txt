French[edit]
Etymology[edit]
From Latin canna (“reed”), from Ancient Greek κάννα (kánna, “reed”), from Akkadian 𒄀 (/qanû/, “reed”), from Sumerian 𒄀𒈾 (gi.na).
Pronunciation[edit]
IPA(key): /kan/

Homophones: cannes, Cannes
Noun[edit]
canne f (plural cannes)
cane; stick
(familiar) peg; leg
rod; fishing rod
Synonyms[edit]
(fishing rod): scion
Derived terms[edit]
canne anglaise
sucre à canne
