She's trapped in the never-ending maze that is her life, and she knows there's no way out...but deep down in her young heart, she longs for something to change. For a certain red-masked turtle, the blazing red of the sunrise reflect his anger. Misunderstood by his family, he feels there's no one in the world that truly understands him, who sees him for the turtle he is behind his mask of fury. Deep down, he doesn't want to be angry-but he doesn't know how to stop. All he can do is pray for a fresh start as he leaves everything behind, that there's enough light within his soul to expel the darkness that threatens to destroy him.When the two cross paths, perhaps a new light will shine on them both. "Thank you for being my sunrise."
