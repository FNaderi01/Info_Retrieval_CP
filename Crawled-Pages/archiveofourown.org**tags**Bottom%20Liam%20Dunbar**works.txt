      After the whole hunter ordeal, Theo now lives across the hall from Liam. Still running from a few spare hunters, Theo and Liam are drawn closer the more they go. Neither of them know what's happening, but slowly realize that their feelings for one another goes further than just friendship. Scott, concerned for Liam's sudden lack of focus, is answered with continuous denies to not only Scott, but to himself that nothing's happened or changed. As the days go by, the only ones left to not realize their bond is Theo and Liam themselves, still in denial.
