		Indonesian[edit]
Etymology[edit]
From Malay menunjuk, from Classical Malay menunjuk. Equivalent to men- +‎ tunjuk.
Verb[edit]
menunjuk
(transitive, literally) point
(transitive, figurative) nominate
(transitive, figurative) elect
