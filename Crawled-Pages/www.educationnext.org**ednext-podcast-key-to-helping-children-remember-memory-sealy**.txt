                                By Education Next                                                                                                                            
		Education Next
EdNext Podcast · Ep. 176 – Oct. 30, 2019: The Key to Helping Children Remember
			Clare Sealy, the head of curriculum and standards for the States of Guernsey and a primary school teacher of 30 years, joins EdNext Editor-in-chief Marty West to discuss the differences between episodic memory and semantic memory, and the keys to each one in helping children remember their lessons in school.
Read “The Best Way to Help Children Remember Things? Not ‘Memorable Experiences’,” which is excerpted from “The researchEd Guide to Educational Myths.”
The EdNext Podcast is available on iTunes, Google Play, Soundcloud, Stitcher and here every Wednesday.
