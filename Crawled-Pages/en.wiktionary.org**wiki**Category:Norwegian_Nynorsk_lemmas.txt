Norwegian Nynorsk lemmas, categorized by their part of speech.
Category:Norwegian Nynorsk adjectives: Norwegian Nynorsk terms that give attributes to nouns, extending their definitions.
Category:Norwegian Nynorsk adverbs: Norwegian Nynorsk terms that modify clauses, sentences and phrases directly.
Category:Norwegian Nynorsk conjunctions: Norwegian Nynorsk terms that connect words, phrases or clauses together.
Category:Norwegian Nynorsk determiners: Norwegian Nynorsk terms that narrow down, within the conversational context, the referent of following noun.
Category:Norwegian Nynorsk interjections: Norwegian Nynorsk terms that express emotions, sounds, etc. as exclamations.
Category:Norwegian Nynorsk morphemes: Norwegian Nynorsk word-elements used to form full words.
Category:Norwegian Nynorsk nouns: Norwegian Nynorsk terms that indicate people, beings, things, places, phenomena, qualities or ideas.
Category:Norwegian Nynorsk numerals: Norwegian Nynorsk terms that quantify nouns.
Category:Norwegian Nynorsk particles: Norwegian Nynorsk terms that do not belong to any of the inflected grammatical word classes, often lacking their own grammatical functions and forming other parts of speech or expressing the relationship between clauses.
Category:Norwegian Nynorsk phrases: Norwegian Nynorsk groups of words elaborated to express ideas, not necessarily phrases in the grammatical sense.
Category:Norwegian Nynorsk prepositions: Norwegian Nynorsk adpositions that are placed before their objects.
Category:Norwegian Nynorsk pronouns: Norwegian Nynorsk terms that refer to and substitute nouns.
Category:Norwegian Nynorsk proper nouns: Norwegian Nynorsk nouns that indicate individual entities, such as names of persons, places or organizations.
Category:Norwegian Nynorsk verbs: Norwegian Nynorsk terms that indicate actions, occurrences or states.
