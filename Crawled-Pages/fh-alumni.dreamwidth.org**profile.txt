                 Mini Bio
                fh_alumni is for the graduated students of Fandom High (alumni), and non-student characters who have left the island but are still in the game (expatriates) to live out their lives away from Fandom.What can be posted here:Correspondence, phone calls, IMs, etc, with alums/expatsOpen posts set off-island, featuring alums/expats interacting with each other and/or islandersPre-played scenes set off-island, featuring alums/expats interacting with each other and/or islandersProse scenes detailing the off-island adventures of alums/expats in their native worldsOOC plotting and planning posts about alum/expat-specific topicsAll of these things can also go in the alum/expat character's personal journal (or fhplotterywheel for plotting); the choice of where to post is up to the player.)A comprehensive information post about the alumni/expat way of life can be found here.
