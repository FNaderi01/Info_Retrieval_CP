    Summary
      Dr John Hamish Watson lay dying on a forgotten battlefield in Afghanistan, until the moon rose, revealing a werewolf picking through the corpses. It turned him,  and the virus it gave him healed the gaping bullet wound in his leg, but now he had a problem. He returned home to London, a full Were, and in desperate need of a better flat.Enter Sherlock Holmes, a Were being killed by his wolf, whose scent makes John's wolf howl inside him. Can he help his new attractive friend, before Sherlock's virus kills him? Could falling in love be the thing that saves them both?
