                 Mini Bio
                Hello and welcome to white_lotus, a community for all things related to the TV shows Avatar: The Last Airbender and the currently airing Legend of Korra. This is a space to post links to your fanworks, graphics, and other creative endeavours, as well as to discuss the show with other fans.rules and guidelines:1. All kinds of fanworks are welcome here, including fic, vids, art, recs, picspam, podfic, icons, meta, and so on. Discussion posts, episode reviews, and links to news stories are also welcome. We also accept multi-fannish crossovers, meta, and discussions that reference Avatar: The Last Airbender and Legend of Korra.2. We welcome works based around all characters, ships, and kinks, and bashing of any those is not accepted. (Saying "I don't like X" is fine; saying "X is disgusting" is not.)3. Posts to this community should follow the posting template. You're welcome to post links to older fanworks/episode reviews/meta and so on, although please restrict these posts to one per day so as not to flood the rlists of our members.4. Please be respectful of other members - debate is encouraged, but personal attacks are not allowed. Please avoid use of sexist, racist, homophobic, transphobic, ablist and other derogatory language.Your mods are eruthros and such_heights, who can be contacted via PM if you have any problems or questions.
