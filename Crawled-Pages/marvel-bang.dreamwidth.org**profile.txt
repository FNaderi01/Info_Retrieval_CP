                 Mini Bio
                Welcome to marvel_bang, a bigbang community focused on producing works of 10,000 words or more for the Marvel Universe.Membership and posting to this community are moderated; only people creating works for the current challenge will be granted access. We will be locking posts for check-ins, updates, and other challenge-specific entries; all signups and finished products will be available to everyone. If you are participating in the challenge, please make sure you join and friend the community, so you get all updates; if you just want to see the finished products, feel free to follow us.Our 2020 schedule can be found here.If you need to contact a mod, please do so through the page-a-mod post.All stories from 2019 and forward are posted on AO3 at: Marvel Big Bang Collection Many older stories can be found in the collection too!Our sister community is dcu_bang.
