                            The Education Exchange: How Do Hot Days Affect Student Learning?
                                By Education Next                                                                                                                            
		Education Next
            School is back in session after one of the hottest summers in recorded history, and many students are now in classrooms without air conditioning. A new study looks at how hotter school days impact. student learning.
Josh Goodman of the Kennedy School at Harvard sits down with Paul E. Peterson to discuss his recent working paper, “Heat and Learning,” co-written with Michael Hurwitz, Jisung Park and Jonathan Smith.
