Hungarian[edit]
Etymology[edit]
kerek (“round”) +‎ -ít (verb-forming suffix)
Pronunciation[edit]
IPA(key): [ ˈkɛrɛkiːt]
Hyphenation: ke‧re‧kít
Rhymes: -iːt
Verb[edit]
kerekít
(transitive) to round (to shape something into a curve)
(transitive, arithmetic) to round (to something: -ra/-re) (to approximate a number)
a kerekítendő szám ― the number to be rounded
Kerekítsd a számot a meghatározott pontosságra. ― Round the number to the predefined accuracy.
(transitive, figurative, of speech, writing) to improvise, extemporize (to create a speech or written text in an impromptu manner)
Synonyms: kapásból készít, rögtönöz
