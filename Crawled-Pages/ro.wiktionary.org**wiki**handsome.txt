engleză
(English)
Etimologie
Din hand + some.
Pronunție
AFI: /'hænsəm/
Adjectiv
handsome (comp. more handsome sau handsomer, sup. most handsome sau handsomest) 
(ieșit din uz) abil, îndemânatic, priceput
For a thief it is so handsome as it may seem it was first invented for him. - Edmund Spenser
(despre un bărbat) arătos, frumos, aspectuos, chipeș
David was a handsome man.
(despre o femeie) elegantă, drăguță
(gener.) corespunzător, potrivit
(p. ext.) generos, nobil, mărinimos
Handsome is as handsome does. - Proverb
(despre o sumă) abundent, amplu, substanțial, considerabil
She gave him a handsome reward.
Sinonime
1: dexterous, handy, skillful
3: elegant, pretty
4: suitable, becoming, appropriate
5: generous
6: ample
Cuvinte derivate
handsomely
Verb
Conjugarea verbului to handsome
Infinitiv
to handsome
Prezent simplupers. 3 sg.
handsomes
Trecut simplu
handsomed
Participiu trecut
handsomed
Participiu prezent
handsoming
(ieșit din uz) a face pe cineva frumos
