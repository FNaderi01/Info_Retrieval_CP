Han character[edit]
兰 (radical 12,  八+3, 5 strokes, cangjie input 廿一一 (TMM), composition ⿱丷三 or ⿱䒑二)

Chinese[edit]
Etymology 1[edit]
For pronunciation and definitions of 兰 – see 蘭 (“orchid; elegant; graceful; etc.”).(This character, 兰, is  the simplified form of 蘭.)
Notes:
Simplified Chinese is mainly used in Mainland China, Malaysia and Singapore.
Traditional Chinese is mainly used in Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.
Etymology 2[edit]
For pronunciation and definitions of 兰 – see 藍 (“blue; indigo plant; Indigofera tinctoria; etc.”).(This character, 兰, is  the draft (1955) first-round simplified and second-round simplified form of 藍.)
Notes:
Simplified Chinese is mainly used in Mainland China, Malaysia and Singapore.
Traditional Chinese is mainly used in Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.
Etymology 3[edit]
For pronunciation and definitions of 兰 – see 籃 (“basket; ring; basket; etc.”).(This character, 兰, is  the second-round simplified form of 籃.)
Notes:
Simplified Chinese is mainly used in Mainland China, Malaysia and Singapore.
Traditional Chinese is mainly used in Hong Kong, Macau, and Taiwan.
