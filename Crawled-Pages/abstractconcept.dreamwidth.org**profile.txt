                 Mini Bio
                Mostly, I write goofy fics. I don't aspire to compose any epic masterpieces. I'm here to have fun. I write lots of chan. I like crossgen with power imbalances. I try hard to warn or at least put an age for the characters.A lot of what I write is tongue-in-cheek. I don't take myself seriously, and I don't expect you to take me too seriously either. I write lots of Snarry and some Draco/Harry and Remus/Harry and Harry/Colin and Lucius/Harry and so forth and so on.
