Khmer[edit]
Etymology[edit]
Compare Thai เพลง (pleeng).
Pronunciation[edit]
Orthographic
ភ្លេងb̥ʰleṅ
Phonemic
ផ្លេង / ភ្លេងp̥ʰleṅ / b̥ʰleṅ
WT romanisation
phleeng, phleing
(standard) IPA(key)
/pʰleːŋ/ ~ /pʰleiŋ/
Noun[edit]
ភ្លេង • (phleeng)
music, melody, song, musical composition
orchestra, band, musical ensemble
