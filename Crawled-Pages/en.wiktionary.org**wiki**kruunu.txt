Finnish[edit]
Etymology[edit]
Older borrowing from Swedish krona (compare German Krone), ultimately from Latin corōna. Doublet with kruuna.
Pronunciation[edit]
IPA(key): /ˈkruːnu/, [ˈkruːnu]
Rhymes: -uːnu
Syllabification: kruu‧nu
