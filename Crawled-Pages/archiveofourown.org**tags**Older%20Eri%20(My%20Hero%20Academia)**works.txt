      Eri's quirk is "rewind", it allows the user to reverse a living individual body to a previous state (doesn't work on non-living objects) and allows the user to let their own body travel backwards in time, because of her quirk she was captured by Overhaul so he can use her powers to fulfill his dream.Midoriya Izuku is a plain boy who later received his quirk called "forward", it allows the user to proceed a living individual body to the next state (doesn't work on non-living objects) and allows the user to let their own body travel forward in time.All for One had a deal with the villain Dragonsbreath (Midoriya Hisashi), but Dragonsbreath couldn't keep it so he decided to kill him and his family as a punishment, after killing Midoriya Hisashi and his wife he saw a small child with green hair and a horn sticking out of the left temple, All for One then gave that child to Overhaul to strenghten their alliance.
