Hindi[edit]
Etymology[edit]
Borrowed from Sanskrit बहुवचन (bahuvacana). Analyzable as बहु (bahu, “many”) +‎ वचन (vacan, “grammatical number”).
Pronunciation[edit]
IPA(key): /bə.ɦʊ.ʋə.t͡ʃən/, [bɔ.ɦɔ.ʋə.t͡ʃə̃n̪]
Noun[edit]
बहुवचन • (bahuvacan) m
(grammar) the plural number; a plural
Antonym: एकवचन (ekavcan)
