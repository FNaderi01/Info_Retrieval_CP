                 Mini Bio
                This is a Spam community/Musebox for members of lastvoyages.Here you may make posts and threads much in the same way as Greatest Hits back on LJ -- relive any flood, any breach, any port, and take whatever directions you wish with it. Old and new characters are allowed provided they have been at or will be at TLV in the future. You may also do memes here, as with the TLV spam community. Adult content must be clearly marked and locked, as well as trigger warnings. Current players are welcome to join. Former players that are still in good standing with the community may also join.
