Fired Heater Risk Ranking Software – FHRisk
Fired heaters are unique in that reliable operation requires input from a variety of disciplines, and they do not fit neatly into standard refinery programs such as RBI. To fill this gap, Becht developed FHRisk to assess the risks of fired heaters and to help identify and prioritize mitigation actions. The tool can be used to prioritize activities on a single heater, or a fleet of heaters across multiple sites.
