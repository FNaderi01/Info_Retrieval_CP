  カノン on Japanese Wikipedia
Pronunciation[edit]
(Tokyo) カ​ノン [káꜜnòǹ] (Atamadaka – [1])IPA(key): [ka̠nõ̞ɴ]
Etymology 1[edit]
Either from English canon or German Kanon, ultimately from Ancient Greek κανών (kanṓn, “measuring rod, standard”).[1]
Noun[edit]
カノン • (kanon) 
a canon
Etymology 2[edit]
Alternative spelling
加農
From Dutch kanon.[2]
Alternative forms[edit]
キャノン (kyanon)
Noun[edit]
カノン • (kanon) 
(weaponry) Synonym of カノン砲 (kanon-hō, “cannon”)
