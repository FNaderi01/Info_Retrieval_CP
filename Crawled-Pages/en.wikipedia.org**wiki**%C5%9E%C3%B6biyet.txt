ŞöbiyetŞöbiyet with Turkish ice cream dondurma on topCourseDessertPlace of originTurkeyMain ingredientsPhyllo dough, nuts, cream Cookbook: Şöbiyet  Media: Şöbiyet
Şöbiyet is a Turkish dessert similar to baklava.[1] It is stuffed with a cream, which is made from milk and semolina, and also nuts (walnut or pistachio). It has a soft but crusty outside and creamy inside.
