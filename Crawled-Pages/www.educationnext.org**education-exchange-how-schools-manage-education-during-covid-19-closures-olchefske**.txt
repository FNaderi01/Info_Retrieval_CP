The Education Exchange · Ep. 138 – April 20, 2020 – How Schools Manage Education During Covid-19 Closures
The former superintendent of Seattle Public Schools, Joseph Olchefske, joins Paul E. Peterson to discuss how to deliver education while physical campuses are closed because of Covid-19, and how to plan simultaneously for either a quick re-opening of school buildings or a prolonged shut-down.
